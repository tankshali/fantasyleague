Game Rules

You need to build your team by selecting 11 players for that particular day's match(s)
You will get the budget of 45 Cr to make your team
Minimum of 1 wicket keeper is required to be part of your team
Minimum of 5 players with bowling skills are required to be part of your team. All-rounders will be considered as a player with bowling skills
Your team will earn points based on performance of players selected in your team. Refer point system details given below
Team selection window closes before 1 hour of start of that day's match

Point System

5 points for each run
70 points for each wicket
50 points for each catch, runout and stumping. Both players involved in runout will get 50 points
100 points bonus if a player scores 100 runs or more. And 50 points bonus if a player scores between 50 and 100 runs
100 points bonus if a player takes 5 wickets or more. And 50 points bonus if a player takes between 3 and 5 wickets
200 points for Man of the match
Points scored by captain player will be multiplied by 2
