Welcome to MFL

Showcase your cricket knowledge by building your own team and compete with your friends

MFL is a free game for cricket lovers and a great way to follow a particular cricket tournament with your friends
You get to build your team by selecting 11 players for that particular day's match and you get points based your team player's actual performance in that match
The real fun is when you create a league among your friend and compete with them
